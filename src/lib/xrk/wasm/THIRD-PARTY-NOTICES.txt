Third-party notices for the bundled AiM XRK/XRZ parser (wasm)
============================================================

`xrk_wasm_bg.wasm` + `xrk_wasm.js` in this directory are a WebAssembly build of
libxrk's pure-Rust core, used by Dove's DataViewer to parse AiM .xrk/.xrz
telemetry files entirely in the browser. Built from source by
scripts/build-xrk-wasm.sh via the wrapper crate in xrk-wasm/ (which depends on
libxrk at the revision pinned in xrk-wasm/Cargo.toml). Redistributed under the
MIT License.
